Who we are

We are a one-stop LAW FIRM offering integrated legal services
with overriding emphasis on client satisfaction.

LITIGATION AND ADR/ARBITRATION :- Our array of advocates are ever ready to attend to matters in all courts of competent jurisdiction in Nigeria on such matters relating to banking, commercial transactions, recovery, intellectual property, contract, labour and employment matters, civil cases such as personal injury, health care law and medical negligence litigations. Our dispute resolution team are lawyers with advocacy experience and offers representation in all types of civil disputes including mediation, arbitration and other form of dispute resolution mechanisms, we are also well-experienced in oil and gas disputes arbitration and enforcement of arbitral awards.

MARITIME AND SHIPPING LAW :- Our maritime law department is well-equiped and trained in all aspect of international maritime litigation practice having handled some briefs and advisory on offshore shipping and marine services transaction. Our commitment to client service coupled with the depth of our legal team consistently gives us referrals and recommendations thereby breaking new grounds.

ENERGY/OIL AND GAS :- Our oil and gas/energy team is abreast with the latest trends in global practice as it relates to arbitration and litigation on transactional work in the energy sector. We represent contractors, sub-contractors, operators and facilitators alike as well as number of service companies right from company incorporation, licences, negotiation stage up to due diligence and perfection of contract.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY,TRADEMARKS,MEDIA AND ENTERTAINMENT :- By combining an awareness of our client’s expectation with a practical and constructive approach we offer an updated opinion on intellectual property, trademarks and copyright issues and seek appropriate redress where an infringement has been perpetrated against their moral and economic interest. Our lawyers are at the forefront of negotiating media and entertainment contracts as our experience spans a broad spectrum of this industry.

CORPORATE COMMERCIAL PRACTICE & COMPANY SECRETARY :- We at Nobilitas provide a range of legal services pertaining to commercial activities by exploiting our in-depth knowledge of the legal framework governing various commercial transactions. We provide exceptional standard in the areas of company registration and secretarial, capital market and statutory compliances with the Companies and Allied Matters Decree and other laws relating to corporate governance, insolvency, financial regulations,securities,and general advisory services.

PROPERTY LAW AND MANAGEMENT :- We offer consultancy services and brokerage in the real estate sector on Sales, Mortgage, Administration of Leases, Finalizing Lease Agreements, Processing and Perfection of title as it relates to real estates. We also undertake Administration of Estates, Probate and Wills.
