Sandra Atabo
B.A (Hons) LL.M (Glasgow), B.L
(Head of IT/Communications)

Sandra Atabo is a graduate of London Metropolitan University where she obtained a B.A (Hons.) Law major degree. She was awarded an LL.M (Distinction) in Information Technology and Telecommunication Law from the University of Glasgow, Scotland. She was called to the Nigerian Bar in 2004.

Sandra has worked as in-house counsel for a number of UK tertiary institutions, namely King's College London (University of London) and Trinity Laban handling data protection, freedom of information, copyright and IT compliance generally.

She worked for the Treasury Department in the Public Law Division (whose major clients were Her Majesty''s Prison Service and Ministry of Defence) prior to her partnership with the Nobilitas Solicitors.
